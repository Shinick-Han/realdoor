# it-005 — T11 — the labeled total band, and the guard that refuses a page of disagreeing stubs

## 1. Target

Backlog **T11** (design it-C of `ocr-extension-design.md`), corpus `confirm`,
`hi_ags_pay_statement_example_2021.pdf`: gross_pay (truth `2,328.01`) and net_pay
(truth `1,511.38`) — both `image_only`. The document is the multiplicity case: five
pages, THREE stub instances printing three different pay periods (p1 the truth
instance; p2 a before/after pair), and the design's hazard 5 says the guard against
that multiplicity is the deliverable even at yield 0.

## 2. The printed evidence (measured: design §A.1 dossier + this iteration's probes)

hi_ags p1's stub prints a summary band as a printed header line of five cells —
`TOTAL GROSS | FED TAXABLE GROSS | TOTAL TAXES | TOTAL DEDUCTIONS | NET PAY` — with
one value line beneath: 2,328.01 under TOTAL GROSS, 444.00 under TOTAL TAXES, 372.63
under TOTAL DEDUCTIONS, 1,511.38 under NET PAY, and the identity
**444.00 + 372.63 + 1,511.38 = 2,328.01 exact**. Why nothing emits today, verified:
the earnings-column total misreads as `'2.328.01'` (parse-refused), so no anchored
run forms and the whole verified/shredded chain is silent; the band is a labeled ROW
structure no existing rule reads. Per-page conduct of the candidate, measured this
iteration: p1 labels each appear exactly once and the band closes; p2's merged
dual-instance stream carries TOTAL TAXES, TOTAL DEDUCTIONS and NET PAY **twice each**
and refuses; p3–p5 print none of the four labels and refuse.

## 3. The license (exactly one of the five): **printed total closed by an arithmetic identity**

The band identity `TOTAL GROSS = TOTAL TAXES + TOTAL DEDUCTIONS + NET PAY` over four
values, each the single number token owned by its own printed header cell. The four
label strings are anchor-only (the `shredded._ROW_LABELS` precedent: they name the
identity's members and never become fields), matched whole-cell, exact after
`normalize_label` with spaces removed — the strip-spaces invariant the OCR engine's
own word-space loss makes necessary (`'TOTALGROSS'` measured), and space removal adds
no character. `FED TAXABLE GROSS` can never match any of the four (whole-cell
equality, no substrings) — that is the design's named adjacency risk, refused
structurally. No corroboration requirement is added beyond the identity: a wrong
admitted value requires two same-delta compensating misreads inside one four-term
equation (measured base rate at 200 dpi: zero digit substitutions, and the one
separator misread parse-refuses), and a corroboration clause fitted to hi_ags's
distribution table would be shape-fitting, not license. Scope: pages that carry
it-003-injected words only, flag **`REALDOOR_OCR_TOTAL_BAND`** (on by default; `=0`
⇒ the new module is never imported and output is byte-identical).

## 4. The rule (one mechanism, new module `core/total_band.py`)

`recover(words, convention, wanted, known_gross)` for one page: (1) every run on the
page whose space-stripped normalized text equals one of the four labels, by role —
any role appearing more than once on the page refuses the page (**the within-page
instance-conflict guard**: a page whose stubs repeat the labels has not said which
band is which); (2) the four cells must sit on one printed line; (3) the value line
is the nearest line below carrying a number token that x-overlaps a cell; on it,
every cell must own exactly one token and each token exactly one cell; (4) all four
tokens must be money in cents-form (two printed decimals — `'2.328.01'`-shaped
misreads never parse); (5) the identity must close at cent grain, with gross > 0,
0 < net ≤ gross, taxes ≥ 0, deductions ≥ 0; (6) a band whose gross disagrees with an
already-answered gross_pay refuses whole. `extract_document` calls it per
OCR-injected page (inside the `_arithmetic_enabled` region, after verified/shredded,
blanks only) and then applies **the cross-page half of the guard**: candidates from
different pages that disagree on any field withdraw everything — `found.setdefault`
page order is never allowed to choose between stub instances. Emissions are
`certainty="low"`, note naming the band identity, `_with_ocr_provenance` appended.

## 5. The hazards, named (each with the refusal that was measured or pinned)

- **The multiplicity page itself** (hazard 5, the point of this iteration): p2's two
  instances merge into one stream (it-003's region merge) and print the labels twice
  → the duplicate-label refusal, measured firing on exactly that page. If a future
  page carried two instances whose labels survived once each, its two value-lines
  under one header line refuse at step (3); if two PAGES each close cleanly on
  different values, the cross-page withdrawal refuses both (pinned as a unit test).
  Residual, named honestly (design blunt-review 5): p1 emits because p2 refuses —
  nothing printed *selects* the truth instance; the guard only guarantees no
  conflicting instance can ever ship a value.
- **FED TAXABLE adjacency**: whole-cell exact match; a value under that cell is owned
  by no band cell and ignored; a token straddling two cells refuses.
- **A misread value surviving the band**: needs two compensating misreads in one
  exact four-term equation; separator misreads (`'2.328.01'`) are not money and
  parse-refuse (cents-form, step 4).
- **Blank forms / prose pages** (kcha's scans, md_labor, the four label-less hi_ags
  pages): no four-label line → refuse. Measured: no change on kcha, up_006, up_014,
  ou, osu, lcc.
- **The pack's rasterized docs** print GROSS PAY / NET PAY but no TOTAL GROSS, TOTAL
  TAXES or TOTAL DEDUCTIONS: the all-four requirement refuses; the sweep must show
  zero pack firings.

## 6. Firing predicate

`loop/falsify/it-005.py` — read-only; carries the candidate conduct verbatim

all 77; fires iff the emitted field set differs; `conflicts` joins every changed
field against truth including `expect_absent`. Sanity: must fire on the target
document and on no pack document.
